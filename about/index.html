About Reaction.

#MarketingFirst

To us, "marketing first" means not having to retool your ecommerce site to add-on features that help you attract and retain customers. Whether it's classic SEO, modern techniques like hashtag tracking, or social sharing on every product, we focus on conversion first.

Real-time Reactive

When a shopper sees inventory changing live on a page it can create a powerful incentive to purchase. Or when a store owner can merchandise products to test their popularity, they can immediately know their conversion results. That’s why we're building everything to be real time.

Designer Friendly

We’re creating a first-of-its-kind platform, built using well known frameworks like jQuery and LESS that many designers and developers already embrace. Add to that the magic of Meteor, and we're making it faster and easier than ever to extend and customize the way you need.

Open Source

We're strong supporters of Open Source and proud to be part of the Open Source and Meteor community. Show your support and interest by following us, or joining our team of contributors at the public repository on Github. Test drive the latest release today at Reaction Commerce.
